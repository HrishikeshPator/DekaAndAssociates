Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about starting and managing your business.

1. I want to start a business. Where should I begin? expand_more

Start with a structured consultation. Before registration, we review:

  • Nature of business
  • Suitable structure (Proprietorship / LLP / Pvt Ltd)
  • GST applicability
  • Estimated compliance cost
  • Required licenses

Registration should follow clarity, not guesswork.

2. Which business structure is better — Proprietorship, LLP or Private Limited? expand_more

It depends on:

  • Number of owners
  • Risk exposure
  • Investment size
  • Funding plans
  • Long-term growth strategy

There is no "best" structure. There is only "suitable".

3. Is GST registration mandatory? expand_more

GST registration depends on:

  • Annual turnover
  • Type of supply
  • Interstate transactions
  • E-commerce activity

We assess eligibility before filing.

4. I received a GST or Income Tax notice. What should I do? expand_more
Do not ignore it. Share the notice immediately. We review, prepare response documentation, and guide further action. Delay increases risk.
5. I am a salaried individual. Do I still need to file Income Tax Return? expand_more

In most cases, yes. ITR may be required for:

  • Income above basic exemption limit
  • Claiming refunds
  • Visa processing
  • Loan applications
  • Capital gains
  • Multiple income sources

Each case is evaluated individually.

6. I run a small business. Do I need regular compliance support? expand_more

Yes, if you have:

  • GST registration
  • Employees
  • Company / LLP structure
  • Regular turnover

Compliance is ongoing, not one-time.

7. Do you help NGOs and Trusts? expand_more

Yes. We assist with:

  • Registration (Society / Trust / NGO – Assam)
  • 12A / 80G registration
  • Project reports
  • Accounting compliance
  • Statutory filings

NGOs must maintain documentation discipline.

8. I am an NRI. Can you handle my tax matters in India? expand_more

Yes. Services include:

  • Income Tax Return filing
  • Capital gains computation
  • Property-related taxation
  • Compliance support

Documentation may be coordinated digitally.

9. Do you prepare project reports and CMA reports for bank loans? expand_more

Yes. We prepare:

  • Detailed Project Reports
  • CMA Reports
  • Financial Projections

Loan approval depends on bank evaluation.

10. Do you provide legal drafting services? expand_more
Yes. Legal documentation and drafting support is facilitated through associated legal professionals in accordance with applicable regulations.
11. Are consultation fees refundable? expand_more
No. Consultation fees are non-refundable. They may be adjustable against confirmed service engagement if agreed in advance.
12. Do you guarantee approval of registrations or licenses? expand_more
No. Approvals are subject to government authority review. We ensure proper documentation and compliance process.
13. How do I proceed? expand_more

Schedule a consultation. Discuss your requirement clearly. Proceed after understanding scope, timeline and fees.

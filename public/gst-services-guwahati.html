GST FAQs

What is the GST registration threshold in Assam? expand_more
For goods suppliers, the threshold is Rs. 40 lakhs. For service providers, it's Rs. 20 lakhs. Interstate sellers and e-commerce operators must register regardless of turnover.
How long does GST registration take? expand_more
Typically 3-7 working days after complete document submission. Additional document requests from the officer may extend the timeline.
What GST returns do I need to file? expand_more
Regular taxpayers file GSTR-1 (by 11th) and GSTR-3B (by 20th) monthly. Composition dealers file quarterly GSTR-4. Annual return GSTR-9 is due by December 31st.

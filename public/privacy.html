1 Introduction

Deka & Associates ("Firm", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of individuals who visit our website or communicate with us.

This website is informational in nature. It does not process financial documents or collect sensitive personal data directly through the website.

2 Nature of Website

The Deka & Associates website:

  • Enables users to contact us

  • Does not collect financial records through online forms

Users are not required to submit tax documents, identity proofs, or financial records through the website.

When you visit or contact us through the website, we may collect:

  • Financial statements

through the website.

Such data is shared through officially communicated channels, not through the website interface.
